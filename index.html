About JJ Galenos Music

Our Mission

We are a non-profit Venezuelan organization dedicated to the promotion, performance and diffusion of Latin American music in all of its diversity.

Our mission as an organization is to bring the art of music to all areas of society and to help bring people together from all stages in life. A journey that originally started in Venezuela a few years back turned into a dream and later became a reality when we opened our operations centre in Toronto on November 25, 2018.

Our Vision

Our vision for the future is to showcase our culture through the art of music not only to the Hispanic community but also to the Anglo-Saxon community and other cultures throughout Canada and the world.

We strive to bring the art of music to all areas of society and to help bring people together from all stages in life. Our mission is to showcase our Latin American roots and bring together people from all cultures, religions and communities in Canada.

Our Founder & Director

Dr. José Javier Blanco Mendez

Twice winner of the Venezuelan National Medical Voice Festival, Dr. Blanco has had a very illustrious musical career both in Venezuela and Canada. Over the years, he has been a member and musical director of various groups including the Carabobo School of Medicine Choir, the Venezuelan organization "Sentir Venezuela" and the prestigious "Upper Canada Choristers" and its "Cantemos" ensemble.

Whether in the operating room or the stage, Dr. Blanco plans to continue following his passions and use his talents to bring people together from all stages in life.
